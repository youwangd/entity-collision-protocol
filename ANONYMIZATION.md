# Anonymized Mirror

This repository is the anonymized mirror of an agent-memory research codebase, prepared for double-blind review at EMNLP 2026 Industry Track.

- All identifying strings (author names, emails, ORCID, original GitHub URLs, employer names) have been redacted from code, configs, READMEs, BibTeX, and PDF metadata.
- Entity-collision test fixtures (e.g. `Acme Corp`, `Veridian Dynamics`, the deliberate `Amazon`/rainforest polysemy entry) are preserved unchanged — they are integral to the protocol and not author-identifying.
- The `paper/dist/engram_v0.2_emnlp.pdf` is the review-mode 48-page PDF (body 6p, ethics + limitations included, references and appendices following).

This mirror will be deprecated and may be deleted after EMNLP 2026 review concludes.
